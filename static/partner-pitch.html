LogicGate Lake Surveys

Partner Pitch: The lake survey report platform for drone operators and environmental consultants.

The problem: You already own a drone and a Part 107 license. You can fly a lake and capture great photos. But turning those photos into a professional, client-ready deliverable is manual, time-consuming, and hard to sell repeatedly.

The opportunity: Wisconsin lake associations and environmental consultants spend money every year on aquatic plant management, shoreline erosion assessment, and water-quality monitoring. They need repeatable, shareable documentation — not just a folder of JPEGs.

What we built

LogicGate Lake Surveys is a multi-tenant SaaS platform that lets you:

  • Create a lake client in under a minute.
  • Schedule and track shoreline surveys.
  • Upload drone images after a flight.
  • Automatically extract GPS coordinates from image EXIF.
  • Generate a branded PDF report with lake metadata, flight details, and an image gallery.
  • Manage multiple lake clients from one dashboard.

Competitive advantage

Built for lakes, not construction

DroneDeploy and Pix4D are generic mapping tools. LogicGate is purpose-built for lake associations, vegetation tracking, and shoreline monitoring.

Recurring revenue, not one-time gigs

Stop selling single photo shoots. Sell spring and fall monitoring subscriptions that generate revenue every year.

Client-ready deliverables

Your clients get a professional PDF with thumbnails, GPS coordinates, and flight notes — ready for board meetings and grant applications.

Low cost, no minimums

No enterprise contracts, no SOC 2 questionnaires, no 100-unit minimums. Start with one lake and scale from there.

What we are doing that nobody else is

  • Vertical-specific reporting: Reports are structured around lake/survey metadata — not generic orthomosaics.
  • EXIF GPS extraction: Every photo is automatically georeferenced without manual tagging.
  • White-label client portal: Your clients log in and see your surveys under your brand.
  • Simple pricing for small operators: No per-project software fees. Predictable monthly cost per lake client.
  • Wisconsin focus: Designed for Lake Country, aquatic invasive species tracking, and DNR grant reporting.

Option B: White-label SaaS subscription

You use the platform under your brand for your own lake clients.

  • Starter: $200/month for up to 5 lake clients.

  • You set your own prices to your clients.

90 days free access. You fly one lake. We generate the report. After the pilot, we co-write a case study and testimonial. No commitment, no credit card.
